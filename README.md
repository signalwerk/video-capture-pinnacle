# Pinnacle Video Transfer

## USB drive requirements

- max. 4GB FAT16 or FAT32
  enough for ~3h of video at 720p/30fps

## Quality settings (Modes)

### Good

Mode: One LED is constantlylit in blue

- Video: 320 × 240 – 768 kbps
- Audio: 64 kbps stereo 48 KHz

### Better

Mode: Two LEDs are constantly lit in blue

- Video: 640 × 480 – 1.2 Mbps
- Audio: 128 kbps stereo 48 KHz

### Best

Mode: All three LEDs are constantly lit in blue

- Video: 720 × 576 (PAL, SECAM) / 480 (NTSC) – 1.5 Mbps
- Audio: 192 kbps stereo 48 KHz
